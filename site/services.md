# {{{ title }}}

Navigating the world of technology can be challenging. I offer consulting
and education services to help you develop deeper understanding and build
sustainable technology practices!

* [For Individuals](#for-individuals)
* [For Students](#for-students)
* [For Businesses](#for-businesses)

My consulting style is one of servitude and active listening. I aim to meet
people where they are without judgment in a way that affirms your individual
and cultural values. I offer my services on a pay-what-you-can basis with a
suggested base rate.

## For Individuals

> _[Schedule an Intake Appointment](https://calendar.app.google/AMnZXKvk3v2sUSQFA) (15 Minutes)_

Smart phones, email, apps, and social media have become an integral part of
most of our lives. Trying to find an effective way to juggle all of these
tools can be daunting. For individuals, I offer personal consulting to help
you identify your human needs and build a strategy for selecting appropriate
tools to meet those needs.

My process:

1. Initial 15 minute consultation (free of charge) to get a general feel for your goals
2. A more in-depth conversation to begin identifying your needs, usually 1-2 hours
3. A period of research where I work to find technology solutions that meet your needs, up to 1 week
4. A tutorial where I help you set up and navigate the tools I have found, usually 1-3 hours.
5. Ongoing support as needed while you adjust to your new workflow!

Suggested rate: $50 per hour (pay what you can!)

## For Students

> _[Schedule an Intake Appointment](https://calendar.app.google/AMnZXKvk3v2sUSQFA) (15 Minutes)_

Learning your first programming language? Need some homework help? I offer
tutoring services for learners of all levels.

I was a Teaching Assistant at CU Boulder in the Computer Science Department. I
have experience with computer history, programming languages, and the theory of
computation. My teaching style is focused on creating an appropriate environment
for learning to take place while acting as a guide rather than an instructor.

Suggested Rate: $25 per hour (pay what you can!)

## For Businesses

> _For intake appointments, please [send me an email](/contact.html) with a brief description of your technology needs._

Every business has unique technology needs. Whether you are just starting out
and need help setting up your e-commerce site, or you have a unique vision for
your company’s internal and external network services, I can offer guidance on
effective and sustainable processes that maximize usability while minimizing
maintenance.

My process:

1. Initial 1 hour consultation to identify your needs and get an understanding of you current tech stack
2. Possibly a period of research where I gather background information on your current tech stack, usually 1 week or less
3. An in-depth interview phase where I meet with internal stakeholders to identify their needs and build a deeper understanding of your unique business, time varies
4. A design phase, where I use what I have learned to architect a customized solution, time varies
5. A formal proposal where I present my findings and recommendations, usually 1-2 hours
6. Ongoing support as needed

Suggested rate: $150 per hour (pay what you can!)
